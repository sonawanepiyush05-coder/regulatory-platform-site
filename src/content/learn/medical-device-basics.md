# Medical Device Basics

Medical devices are products used for diagnosis, prevention, monitoring, or treatment of diseases.

## Examples

- Syringe
- Stethoscope
- Pacemaker
- MRI
- Ventilator

## Device Life Cycle

- Design
- Development
- Manufacturing
- Distribution
- Post Market Surveillance
